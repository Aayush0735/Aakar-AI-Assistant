[Center Name] Academy — Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: What exams do you prepare students for?
A: We offer comprehensive coaching for JEE Mains, JEE Advanced, NEET-UG, and MHT-CET. We also have foundation programs for students in Class 8-10 to build a strong base for these competitive exams.

Q: What is the admission process?
A: Students can visit our center for a free counseling session. We conduct a diagnostic test to assess the student's current level, followed by a one-on-one discussion with our academic counselors. Based on the test results, we recommend the most suitable batch and may also offer scholarships.

Q: Is there a scholarship available?
A: Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships ranging from 10% to 100% fee waiver. The scholarship is determined based on performance in our entrance test. Toppers from school board exams (90%+ marks) are also eligible for special discounts.

Q: What if I miss a class?
A: All our classes are recorded and uploaded to our student portal within 24 hours. Students can access recorded lectures anytime. Additionally, we conduct makeup classes every Sunday for students who missed regular sessions.

Q: Do you provide study material?
A: Yes, we provide comprehensive study material designed in-house by our faculty. This includes theory notes, solved examples, practice problems (topic-wise and difficulty-graded), previous year question papers with solutions, and formula sheets. The material is updated annually to reflect the latest exam patterns.

Q: How are doubts resolved?

Q: What is your success rate?
A: Our track record speaks for itself — 95% of our students clear JEE Mains cutoff, and we've had 500+ IIT selections in the last 5 years. For NEET, 300+ students have scored above 600 in the past 3 years.

Q: Can I join mid-year?
A: Yes, we accept mid-year admissions. We provide bridge courses and extra support to help students catch up with the batch. Our academic counselors will create a customized study plan for late joiners.

Q: Do you offer online classes?
